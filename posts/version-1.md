---
date: 2020-05-24
title: Announcing Version 1.0.0 of the PolyForm Licenses
---

The PolyForm Project is pleased to announce 1.0.0 versions of its first licenses:

<a href="/licenses/noncommercial/1.0.0">PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0</a> is a general noncommercial license for software.

<a href="/licenses/perimeter/1.0.0">PolyForm Perimeter 1.0.0</a> allows use and distribution, but not in a manner competitive with the software.

<a href="/licenses/defensive/1.0.0">PolyForm Defensive 1.0.0</a> allows use and distribution, but not in a manner competitive with the licensor of the software.

<a href="/licenses/strict/1.0.0">PolyForm Strict 1.0.0</a> removes permission to distribute copies and make changes, leaving only permission to use for noncommercial purposes.

<a href="/licenses/internal-use/1.0.0">PolyForm Internal Use 1.0.0</a> permits use for internal business purposes.

<a href="/licenses/small-business/1.0.0">PolyForm Small Business 1.0.0</a> permits use by small businesses and other small organizations.

<a href="/licenses/free-trial/1.0.0">PolyForm Free Trial 1.0.0</a> permits a free, time-limited trial.

These licenses represent the hard work and feedback of too many contributors to name, over a period of eleven months.
