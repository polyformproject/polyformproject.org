## Licenses

PolyForm Project publishes a suite of standardized licenses:

<img width="191" height="191" src="/noncommercial.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/noncommercial/1.0.0">PolyForm Noncommercial</a> is a general noncommercial license for software.

<img width="191" height="191" src="/perimeter.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/perimeter/1.0.0">PolyForm Perimeter</a> permits uses other than those that compete with the software.

<img width="191" height="191" src="/shield.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/shield/1.0.0/">PolyForm Shield</a> permits uses other than those that compete with the provider of the software.

<img width="191" height="191" src="/strict.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/strict/1.0.0">PolyForm Strict</a> removes permission to distribute copies and make changes, leaving only permission to use for noncommercial purposes.

<img width="191" height="191" src="/internal.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/internal-use/1.0.0">PolyForm Internal Use</a> permits use for internal business purposes.

<img width="191" height="191" src="/small-business.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/small-business/1.0.0">PolyForm Small Business</a> permits use by small businesses and other small organizations.

<img width="191" height="191" src="/free-trial.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/free-trial/1.0.0">PolyForm Free Trial</a> permits a free, time-limited trial.

<img width="191" height="191" src="/empty.png" alt="logo"><a href="https://polyformproject.org/licenses/countdown/1.0.0">PolyForm Countdown</a> helps developers schedule changes to different license terms in the future.
